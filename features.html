About Us

I believe that taking care of your feet shouldn't be a luxury but a necessity.

Affordable Foot Care

Foot care is essential for maintaining an active and independent lifestyle. I am committed to providing affordable tailored specifically to meet the needs of seniors in our community.

Comprehensive Care

Not only do I offer affordable prices, but as a skilled foot care nurse, my unwavering commitment lies in providing comprehensive foot care services which include the following:

  • Complete Foot Assessment
  • Diabetic Foot Care and Neuropathy Screening
  • Trimming and filing of toenails
  • Treatment of Corns and Callouses
  • Reduction of Thickened Nails , Fungal Nails, and Education on Treatment Options
  • Ingrown Nail Care
  • Customized Treatment Plan
  • Referrals made as required

Accessible Foot Care

I believe that every individual should have access to high-quality and affordable foot care. With my services readily available to you at affordable prices, there's no longer a need to postpone foot care or rely on transportation from busy family members.

Continuity of Care

I understand the importance of continuity of care. Clients look forward to seeing the same smiling face and trusted service.

As a sole proprietor who is passionate about her work I am dedicated to ensuring all of your foot care needs are met with the thorough attention your feet deserve.
